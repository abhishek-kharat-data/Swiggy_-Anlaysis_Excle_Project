## Project Overview
The Swiggy Business Intelligence Project aims to analyze customer purchases, product performance, and delivery efficiency using excle. The dashboard provides insights into sales trends.
By integrating data from multiple sources such as customers, products, sales , orders , and ratings, the project enables data-driven decision-making.  monitor KPIs, and improve overall operational efficiency.
## Dataset Description
The Swiggy  dataset contains comprehensive information on customers, products, orders, transactions, delivery partners, and ratings. It provides a 360° view of the business, enabling analysis of sales performance, customer satisfaction, and delivery efficiency.
State : Represents the Indian state where the order was placed.
 City  :City from which the food order was made.
 Order Date :The exact date when the order was placed.
 Day : Day of the week corresponding to the order date.
 Quarter : Financial quarter of the year in which the order was placed.
 Week : Week number of the year when the order was placed.
Restaurant Name : Name of the restaurant from which food was ordered.
 Location : Area/locality of the restaurant or delivery location.
 Category : Type of food category offered by the restaurant.
## Business Objectives
 Analyze customer order trends by date and time
 Identify popular restaurants, dishes, and categories
 Understand location-wise food demand
 Track seasonal and quarterly sales patterns
  Improve recommendations, promotions, and delivery efficiency

## Tools & Techniques Used
Tool:
Microsoft Excle— Used for data cleaning, transformation, and creating interactive dashboards to visualize Swiggy sales data effectively.
Key Insights
Sales Trends:
 Orders are concentrated in specific weeks and quarters
Few restaurants receive the majority of orders
Veg and North Indian dishes are highly preferred
 Certain locations show consistently higher demand
 Recommended items drive more customer orders
